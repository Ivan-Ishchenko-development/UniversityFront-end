Body to Body Massage

The body to body massage, also known as melee or nuru massage, is a very sensual massage performed by two people, usually a masseuse and a client. It has fantastic powers and its main objectives is to provide relaxation and pleasure.

Relaxing Anti-stress Massage

The relaxing anti-stress massage helps to calm the body and mind.  Relaxing massage improves blood circulation, increases the flow of nutrients, removes catabolites and metabolites (toxic substances from cells) harmful to the body, besides relieving pain and facilitating muscle activity

Four-hand Massage

The four-hand massage uses the same movements on each side of the body, for each professional, that is, each on one side of the body with the pressure of the hands equally applied.  Four-hand massage seeks to combine different styles of massage.

Sports Massage

Sports massage is a technique indicated for amateur or professional athletes, as it works with the manipulation and rehabilitation of the soft tissues of the body, including muscles, ligaments and tendons.

Hot Stone Massage

Hot stone massage combats muscle pain and stress, also helps to make a body detox.  The hot stone massage is a massage made with hot basalt stones all over the body, including the face and head, which helps to relax and relieve the stress accumulated during daily tasks.

Ayurvedic Massage

Ayurvedic massage is a type of massage known for its preventive virtues and the well-being it provides.  Ayurvedic massage is inspired by Ayurveda (life science) and traditional Indian massage.  this technique applies muscle kneading and sliding movements performed not only with the hands but also with the use of the feet ending with walking on the client's back.
